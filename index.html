About Me

Gaming has always been a passion of mine and it is long past time that my work life takes a step in the direction of my passion. I can’t wait to begin working in game industry because it is something I have wanted for some time now and is an exciting career in my eyes.

I have recently finished college gaining a Computer Science degree specifically in computer information systems. My focus is Web Game Programming. I maintained a 4.0 while working a full-time job and spending time with my family. Titles are all well and good, but my reasoning for going to college in my 30’s this is because I love video games. When I think about what type of job I want to wake up and do every day, it is creating video games. Ultimately, I want to work on the core programming that make the games function. Things like game play mechanics and A.I.

Check out my Portfolio for past work and check out my Blog to see what I am up to now.
